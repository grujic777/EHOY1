Grain implementation

When you arrive at our place, we will conduct a conversation where we will refer you to the procedure. After that we give you local anesthesia in the area behind the ears. With a small incision, we insert a bead that automatically connects to the nerves of the eye and brain. There is also a strip in the bead that, with the help of the brain, stores everything that has ever been seen through the lens.

Special lenses

The lenses are specially made with 30 mini cameras that capture every corner of your view. Through small needles through the pupil they send the recorded material through the brain into memory. Which is stored and when called by the controller returns a specific image, recording and audio. You can use the controls to select any memory that comes to mind. Reverse it, pause it , and watch it .

Remote control

The remote is made like any other we are familiar with so far. There is a separate button for each option which will be explained later in the guide. All you need to do is connect to a specific screen device and let a movie of your memories play out in front of your eyes.Each remote has a 2-year warranty. If you lose it, be sure to contact us so that we can disable it and save you a new one, at an additional cost of course. Each remote has its own ID by which you are connected and entered into the user database.

Guide thru remote

As mentioned each remote has its own ID through which you are connected. Therefore, each remote is special in a certain way. They are also very easy to use. There are a couple of commands through which many functions are performed. They are as follows:
On/Off button :
Used to turn on and connect to the nearest screen or screen in sight.
Select button :
Used to select the recordings you want to play. And later for rewinding. It is circular in shape and sensitive to touch.
Play button :
After connecting to the screen and selection allows you to play the memory you selected.

Back button :
It takes you back to the memory menu. It can also be used as a general return button during more complex operations.
Audio button
Serves up and down audio sound during broadcast. It is also sensitive to touch. You can change the sound of your recording with just one swipe up or down.
Zoom button :
Another touch button. Serves with video zoom. Move up or down to zoom in on the image. And left and right are used for coordination on the screen.

Screen

Our company also offers all types of broadcasting devices. The most popular among them is a tablet that is automatically associated with your remote ID. So you don't have to connect, but you can automatically see your memory every time you turn on the tablet.
Screens are not included in the price and are ordered separately. Each screen has its own commands that make it easier to use the package only at a slightly higher price.
